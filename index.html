About Us

CodeBuddy Africa

CodeBuddy Africa is a tech education initiative founded to empower high school students across Africa by teaching them how to code and equipping them with essential digital and soft skills for the future. It is structured as a tech company and community platform that partners with schools throughout the continent to deliver hands-on, practical training in web development and other digital skills.

Read More

Mission & Vision

To build a new generation of African innovators by introducing coding at the high school level.

Target Audience

High school students across Africa, with a focus on inclusivity and accessibility regardless of background or location.

Curriculum Design

Offers a 12-week structured curriculum in web development, including: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Bootstrap 5. Each week features a detailed teaching note and accompanying PowerPoint slide decks.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to commonly asked questions about CodeBuddy Africa.

Who can join CodeBuddy Africa?

Any high school student with interest in technology, coding, or digital creativity is welcome! No prior coding experience is needed.

Where is CodeBuddy Africa located?

We currently partner with secondary schools across Nigeria and other parts of Africa. Some of our programs are held in schools, while others may be online or in coding bootcamps.

How do I enroll my child or school in the program?

You can contact our team directly for a quick sign up. If you’re a school administrator, you can request a partnership via our direct contact line. We will attend immediately.
